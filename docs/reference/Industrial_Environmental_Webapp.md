
<u>The Concept:</u>  
- An enterprise B2B marketplace and vendor network platform for industrial facility managers and environmental health and safety professionals to discover, vet, and source trusted environmental compliance consultants and service providers across the Pacific Northwest. Facilities use it as a curated directory to quickly request quotes and compare qualified providers.

<u>Specific features</u> 
-  Vendors create trust networks to assist in pre-vetting so facilities can ask their existing vendors about them if they see that they are networked in the app.
- You can just email the app and ask for referrals and have them land in your inbox.
- A human only space?
- Can schedule meetings in the platform. Share calendar/select free days /times.
- 

<u>Monetization Method Options:</u>
- Vendors pay a monthly subscription to maintain a verified profile with service offerings, certifications, and client reviews.
- Facility managers pay a monthly subscription for access. 


<u>Who is it for?</u>
- Industrial facility/commercial property managers who hate sales.
- Industrial facilities for commercial property owners that just received new permits and need vendors to help them comply with the permit 

<u>The Pitch: </u>
- Industrial facility managers spend weeks finding and vetting environmental compliance vendors through fragmented channels like Google searches and word of mouth. Our platform solves this by creating a pre-vetted network where environmental consultants and service providers pay to maintain verified profiles. Facility managers get one trusted place to find specialists for stormwater permits, dangerous waste compliance, air quality, refrigerant management, and other environmental services. We vet vendors upfront, so facilities spend less time on procurement and more time on compliance.

<u>Adoption Drivers for Vendors:</u>
- Helps Sales: Facilities can post RFQ’s on the site for the vendors to bid on. Can land in their email inbox. 

<u>Adoption Drivers for Facility Managers:</u>
- Solves vendor sourcing. Time saving.
- Solves vetting - Pre-qualification features. Bridges vendor trust. 
- Built in quoting feature for Vendors. 
- Built in meeting scheduler.
- Solves saturated email inboxes - Can DM vendors directly in app.
- No automation / chatbots. Speak directly with people.
